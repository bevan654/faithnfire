Facilities

The tournament will be held in the school's indoor gymnasium featuring multiple volleyball courts, spectator seating, and amenities. Change rooms and bathrooms are available on site.

Parking

Free parking is available on site in the school car park. Overflow parking is available on surrounding streets. Carpooling is encouraged.

Getting There

Reynella East College is located approximately 25 minutes south of Adelaide CBD via the Southern Expressway. Exit at Reynella and follow signs to Myrtle Road. Public transport is available via bus routes servicing the Old Reynella area.
