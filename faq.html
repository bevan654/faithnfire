FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Faith & Fire Invitational?
The Faith & Fire Invitational is a one-day volleyball tournament bringing together parish and church communities from across Adelaide for a day of sport, fellowship, and faith. It is organised by SMYM Adelaide South.
Who can participate?
The tournament is open to teams from any Christian parish, church, or youth group in Australia. All skill levels are welcome — the focus is on community and fun as much as competition.
How many players per team?
Each team must register between 6 and 10 players. Six players are on court at any time, with unlimited substitutions. Mixed teams are welcome and encouraged.
What should I bring?
Wear comfortable sportswear and clean indoor court shoes (non-marking soles). Bring a water bottle, towel, and any knee pads or braces you use. Team uniforms are encouraged but not required.
Is there a cost to enter?
The registration fee is $225 per team for SA-based teams and $175 per team for interstate teams.
Are spectators welcome?
Absolutely! Family, friends, and parish members are all welcome to come and cheer on their teams. There is no entry fee for spectators.
What denomination is this event for?
While organised by SMYM (Syro Malabar Youth Movement), the Faith & Fire Invitational welcomes teams and individuals from all Christian denominations. Unity through faith and sport is at the heart of this event.
Will food be provided?
Food will be provided for registered players. Spectators and other attendees will be able to purchase food on the day.
